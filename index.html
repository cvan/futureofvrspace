The Future of VR Space

Critic-Free Atmosphere

We want passion and enthusiasm.

To quote the father of brainstorming and advertising extraordinare Alex Osborn:

  1. Don’t judge or criticize ideas.
  2. Be freewheeling. The wilder the idea, the better.
  3. Go for quantity. The more ideas you have, the better.
  4. Build on the ideas of fellow group members.

Now that we are freed from the shackles of judgement, creativity and innovation shall become the principal benefits.
